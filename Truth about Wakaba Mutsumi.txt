=== Basic Information & Profile ===
Wakaba Mutsumi is the rhythm guitarist of the band Ave Mujica, performing under the stage name Mortis. Mutsumi is a 15-year-old first-year high school student at Tsukinomori Girls' Academy. Born on January 14 (Capricorn), Mutsumi stands at 153 cm tall. Mutsumi is the former guitarist of the disbanded band CRYCHIC and a childhood friend of Toyokawa Sakiko. Mutsumi has played the guitar ever since Mutsumi was young and as a result is a skilled guitarist. In middle school, after watching Morfonica's live together with Sakiko, Mutsumi was invited by Sakiko to join Sakiko's band, CRYCHIC.

Mutsumi is a very stoic girl who constantly has trouble expressing Mutsumi's self properly. While Mutsumi generally means well and also has a caring side to Mutsumi, Mutsumi's way of wording things can sometimes make Mutsumi come off as rude or insensitive instead. Due to Mutsumi's past, Mutsumi hates being in the spotlight solely over Mutsumi's status as the daughter of two celebrities. Mutsumi is a member of the Tsukinomori gardening club, where Mutsumi grows cucumbers, tomatoes, and eggplants.

Mutsumi is a girl of slightly below-average height. Mutsumi has straight, ashy, light green hair that goes past Mutsumi's shoulders and a straight-cut fringe, typically adorned with a small white barrette. As Mutsumi, Mutsumi's casual attire consists of a navy blue plaid dress with an off-white ruffled collar, along with a teal bow and black choker with white ruffles. Mutsumi's footwear consists of off-white ankle socks and dark brown mary janes. As Mortis, Mortis, like all Ave Mujica members, wears a mask to conceal Mortis's identity, wearing a lace mask that covers the entirety of Mortis's face minus Mortis's eyes. The flowers depicted on Mortis's mask are chrysanthemums. Mortis wears a red beret, a loose off-white shirt with translucent sleeves, along with a black translucent shawl with red lapels and a black ruff, along with an off-white jabot collar adorned with a muted green gem. On Mortis's lower body Mortis wears an opened black skirt along with red shorts adorned with gold buttons, and black tights with black ankles adorned with black ribbons and red shoes.

Mutsumi has a 7-string heavy metal guitar, specifically the SCHECTER EX-24-7st Ave Mujica Proto Model. Mutsumi has been practicing guitar since childhood.

=== Family Background ===
Mutsumi's father is Takafumi Wakaba, a popular comedian. Mutsumi's mother is Minami Mori, a famous actress. Mutsumi lives in a massive house that is frequently visited by celebrities and socialites.

=== Origin of the "Mortis" Persona ===
The stage name "Mortis" is derived from "Lacus Mortis", symbolizing death. Mortis's mask features a daisy motif to reflect this meaning. As a rhythm guitarist, Mortis brings depth and mystery to the band.
Growing up in the spotlight as the child of celebrities, Mutsumi had almost no personal space. To cope with social situations, Mutsumi started playing different "roles" from the age of three: a quiet, doll-like girl at home, and a sociable girl outside. However, once Mutsumi discovered the guitar as something "truly Mutsumi's own," the quiet "Mutsumi" became Mutsumi's host personality, and the other roles faded.

=== Middle School Era (CRYCHIC) ===
In middle school, Mutsumi and Sakiko watched a Morfonica concert at the Tsukinomori Music Festival. Sakiko immediately decided to form a band with Mutsumi, leading to the birth of CRYCHIC.
Later, due to sudden family issues, Sakiko decided to quit Tsukinomori school and the band. When Sakiko announced Sakiko's departure in the studio, Mutsumi, who was secretly struggling with Mutsumi's inability to make Mutsumi's guitar "sing," delivered a fatal blow to the band by saying: "I never thought playing in a band was fun..."
As Sakiko ran away crying in the rain, Mutsumi stood holding an umbrella but could not take a step to chase Sakiko. Since then, Mutsumi blamed Mutsumi's self for CRYCHIC's destruction and carried a deep sense of guilt towards Sakiko.

=== High School Spring/Summer Era ===
Mutsumi entered Tsukinomori Girls' Academy alongside Nagasaki Soyo. Mutsumi kept in touch with Sakiko but, following Sakiko's wishes, refused to reveal Sakiko's situation to Soyo.
When Soyo formed a new band (MyGO!!!!!) and invited Mutsumi, Mutsumi remained indifferent. Later, Mutsumi secretly attended MyGO!!!!!'s concert. After the show, Mutsumi left a gift of homegrown cucumbers packaged in a chocolate bag in the dressing room. Soyo chased after Mutsumi and asked how the concert was. Mutsumi replied, "It was great," which angered Soyo. Soyo rejected and returned the cucumbers.
Eventually, Sakiko invited Mutsumi to join a new band. Mutsumi felt that Sakiko was "about to break." Mutsumi agreed and joined Ave Mujica. Mutsumi performed in Ave Mujica's first live (Primo dia in scaena) under the code name Mortis.

=== High School Fall/Winter Era: The Psychological Breakdown ===
After Ave Mujica's Budokan live (Nostri Nosctis), the band's keyboardist, Utenji Nyamu (Amoris), suddenly exposed Mutsumi's face to the public. Mutsumi's identity as the daughter of famous celebrities was leaked, causing immense media pressure. Mutsumi's mother neglected Mutsumi, and Mutsumi's private basement rehearsal room was exposed. Mutsumi began experiencing hyperventilation and hallucinations.
During the first stop of Ave Mujica's national tour (Continuatio Festi), Mutsumi collapsed like a broken puppet on stage. The pressure completely broke Mutsumi.

=== The Awakening of Mortis (Dissociative Identity) ===
Caught between the Ave Mujica band members' arguments and Mutsumi's own despair, Mutsumi suffered a dissociative identity breakdown. The protective "doll" persona from Mutsumi's childhood took over Mutsumi's body. Mutsumi surrendered Mutsumi's mind and body to this protector, who officially identified the persona as "Mortis".
Unlike Mutsumi, the Mortis persona is talkative, theatrical, and highly sociable. Mortis treats Mutsumi as a separate person ("little Mutsumi") who is sleeping inside Mortis.
Mortis interacts smoothly with other band members and easily becomes popular. However, Mortis actively hates Sakiko, blaming Sakiko for forcing Mutsumi into a deep slumber. Mortis explicitly told Sakiko: "It is Sakiko's fault that Mutsumi is sleeping. Mutsumi will never come back to see Sakiko."

=== The Conflict Between Mutsumi and Mortis ===
When Mutsumi's consciousness temporarily awakened after hearing Takamatsu Tomori's band play "Mayoiuta", Mutsumi and Mortis argued violently in Mutsumi and Mortis's inner mental world. Mutsumi wanted to see Sakiko, but Mortis refused.
In the real world, Mortis displayed erratic behavior, wandering around and talking to Mortis's guitar as if the guitar were a phone, begging a "doctor" to wake Mutsumi up. Soyo discovered Mortis in this tragic state and spent days taking care of Mortis.
Eventually, Sakiko demanded that Mutsumi reform CRYCHIC. However, Mortis rejected reforming CRYCHIC, pushing instead to keep Ave Mujica alive, because Mortis believed Mortis would cease to exist if Ave Mujica disbanded.

=== The Resolution and Merging of Personas ===
During a final confrontation in Mutsumi and Mortis's inner world, Mutsumi accidentally "killed" the Mortis persona by taking the guitar from Mortis, causing Mortis to fall into the abyss. Mortis realized that as a protector, Mortis could not exist without Mutsumi. To survive, Mortis decided to fully integrate and "play the role of Wakaba Mutsumi."
During Ave Mujica's return live (Auferte memoriam vestram), Mortis found Mutsumi in the abyss of Mutsumi's imagination and handed the guitar back to Mutsumi. The two personas successfully merged. Mutsumi began incorporating both Mutsumi's exceptional guitar skills and Mortis's high-level theatrical acting into Mutsumi's performances.

=== Trivia & Traits ===

Wakaba: Waka means "young" and Ba means "leaf."

Mutsumi means "harmony" or "unity"

Mutsumi loves cucumbers. Mutsumi grows cucumbers at school, uses a cucumber flower as Mutsumi's chat avatar, and has a growing cucumber as Mutsumi's phone wallpaper.

Mutsumi loves mango juice and is almost always seen drinking mango juice instead of other beverages.

Mutsumi takes piano lessons at Tsukinomori, aside from playing guitar.

Mutsumi's speech is extremely brief. For example, Mutsumi once told Sakiko to move by simply saying: "Saki, move." (祥、移動). Despite Mutsumi's short sentences, Mutsumi often brutally points out truths that others refuse to face.
